Eudes Correia is a Brazilian watercolorist.
He transforms ordinary people who travel in the city, almost invisible, into the protagonists of his work.
Always counting a history with sensibility and personality.
Eudes excelled on the international stage, participated in exhibitions, and gave workshops in various countries around the world.
